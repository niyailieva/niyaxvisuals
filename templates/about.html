ABOUT THE BRAND

About

NIYAXVISUALS is my way of turning cars into feelings: motion, texture, light, and the atmosphere around builds.

NIYAXVISUALS

NIYAXVISUALS is a creative automotive photography brand focused on mood, movement, and personality. The goal is not only to show cars clearly, but to make them feel alive in the frame.

Hi, I’m Nia

Like most photographers, I started shooting on my phone. I kept photographing everything around me, experimenting constantly, and learning by doing.

After that I spent years working with Canon cameras, and now I shoot on Sony. The equipment changed over time, but the most important thing I developed was my eye: I started seeing frames in everything, even when I was not holding a camera.

My style is not only cinematic. I also enjoy old-school cyber and grungy visual language. I like grain, contrast, strong reflections, unusual crops, and images that feel slightly imperfect in a good way.

What the brand stands for

NIYAXVISUALS combines cinematic photography with a more experimental edge. Some shoots are clean and atmospheric, others are rougher, more digital, more distorted, or more VHS-inspired.

The brand is built around capturing not just the car itself, but the full moment around it: the location, the mood, the lighting, and the feeling the build gives off.
